It's me!

I enjoy expanding my repetoire of Unity's systems and learning new aspects of game development. I'm currently exploring Unity's shader system through shader graph and hand written code.

Land Under Rot

  • The Studio

    Code Rot was formed through Fitchburg State University's Game Studio internship program. This is entirely student run and serves as the Game Design major's capstone.

  • The Game

    You play as a small boy that falls down a rotting tree stump to rescue your sister, but at the bottom you discover an entire world inhabited by plant organisms. You quickly realize that you will be turned into mulch in this world unless you fashion a disguise, so you place a seed on your head and go out into the land under rot. Hop on mushrooms, break bark with your hard seed head and interact with the plant organisms as an equal to find the whereabouts of your sister in this cute but twisted world.

  • Role: Programmer

    For Land Under Rot, I started out as one of the two programmers working on the player controller, interactions, and dialogue system. I would later expand my focus to our shader, Github management, animation controller's logic and implementation, and general Unity implementation.
